ROCK PAPER SCISSORS

INSTRUCTION

Each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an open hand:
rock (a fist), paper (an open hand), or scissors (a fist with the index and middle fingers extended).
Rock crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, and paper covers rock, determining the winner of each round.
The game is typically played in a best-of-three format, with players counting their wins to determine the overall winner.
Make your choice quickly, and have fun competing in this simple yet classic hand game!
